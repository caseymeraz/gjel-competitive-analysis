Competitive Gaps

Where GJEL Is Getting Beaten or Is Absent

Markets and keywords where competitors outrank GJEL or where GJEL has no presence at all.

San Francisco

Dolan (#1-2) and Walkup (#2-4) own this market. GJEL barely registers despite having an SF page.

Sacramento

AutoAccident.com dominates completely. Walkup is #2 for truck/motorcycle. GJEL only captures Sacramento traffic through blog posts about local accidents, not service pages.

"Bay Area" Keywords

Walkup (#1-3) and Dolan (#1-3) own "bay area personal injury attorney/lawyer" and similar. GJEL doesn't target these terms at all.

Missing City Markets

AutoAccident.com covers Napa, Truckee, Redding, Eureka, Merced, Citrus Heights, Roseville, and Brentwood. GJEL has no presence in any of them.

"California Wrongful Death Attorney/Lawyer"

Walkup is #1, GJEL is #17. This is a high-value keyword (1,400 volume) where GJEL is underperforming despite strong wrongful death settlement content.

San Jose

GJEL ranks #5-10 for most San Jose terms. Walkup is #2 for "san jose personal injury attorney." Neither firm dominates, but GJEL should own this market given their office location.
